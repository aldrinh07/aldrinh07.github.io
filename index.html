About

Self-taught, Freelance Web Developer who also just recently graduated from Dev Mountain Web Development Bootcamp (april 2015) and Looking for a Full-time Opportunity.

        After graduating from DevMountain, I started working for a start-up Company (Guesterly) as a Contracting Jr. Web Developer, and Just recently (June, 2015) started to work for another start-up (Xpayable) as a Contracting(part-time-flex) Web Developer.

        During this part of my Web Development Journey, I was still working for the United States Postal Service as a window clerk but because of my desire to code and love for Web Development, I decided to go ALL IN and resign from the Post Office(June, 2015) to be able to code full-time, continue growing and learning as I seek for my first Full-time Employment as a Web Developer! I then recieved another opportunity to work part-time as a Mentor for DevMountain Web Development Bootcamp.

        I BELIEVE in being HONEST and TRUE in all things. I do not want to fake that I know all things web development. I am starting to feel pretty comfortable with AngularJS, HTML, CSS. I know basic javascript and still continuing to learn more. the backend that I am familiar with right now is Firebase, Its not much, But looking back from how I have progressed and what I learned in Only 12 weeks time from Dev Mountain. I know that I can Learn Fast and will learn the things needed in able to accomplish the tasks that Ive been given.

        What Drives Me?... My Family. I am Married and have a 5-month old girl and I want to be successful because of them, not just for myself. though I have passion for Web Development and enjoy Coding, It is for this purpose that I work harder than I do, and it is where I find more motivation to be the best I can be.

        I love to keep a Positive perspective. I can work well and communicate well with others, and I love to Learn and Progress in all aspects of life.
